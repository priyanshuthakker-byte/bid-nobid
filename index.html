MSME EMD Rules
• Nascent is MSME (UDYAM-GJ-01-0007420) — exempt from EMD under PPP-MSME Gazette SR.No.462/2012
• Attach EMD Exemption Letter on company letterhead signed by authorised signatory with each bid
• Where BG required: validity = Bid validity + 60 days · only scheduled bank · not cooperative
• EMD refund follow-up: within 15 days of L1 announcement for non-L1 bidders
